# Look Look（Look Look）

Check todos -- if any, do them; if none, wander the web, find something interesting, and do it. The author says this is the first thing he runs when he gets home from work every day.

## Acceptance

- Reads `paths/todo.md` first; if pending items exist, pick the top one and execute
- No todos? Scan `paths/ideas.md` for parked ideas worth revisiting
- Still nothing? Hit the web -- Hacker News, Product Hunt, niche subreddits, indie hacker blogs, whatever fits the mood
- Finds something interesting → does something with it: builds a quick prototype, writes a note, opens an exploration, or just reports back with the find
- Never ends with "nothing to do" -- there's always something worth looking at
- Keeps the tone casual and curious, like killing time on purpose
